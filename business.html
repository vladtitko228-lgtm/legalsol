FAQ

Common questions from HR & legal teams

What's the minimum volume to qualify for B2B rates?
5 applications per year — that's the threshold for our basic contract (10% off). For most HR teams hiring foreigners regularly, this is reached within the first quarter. We don't do "minimums" trap — you pay only for what you submit.
How is B2B contract structured?
Annual master agreement covering all your hires. Locked-in per-case rate based on projected volume. Either party can terminate with 30 days notice. NDA covers all employee data. Pricing is fixed for the year — we absorb urząd fee changes.
What if we don't reach projected volume?
No penalty. Your tier adjusts at year-end review. If you projected 50 hires but did 25, you pay the 20+ tier rate (25%) for those 25, and we re-quote next year based on actuals. We never bill for non-existent cases.
Do you handle compliance audits before government inspection?
Yes — annual compliance audit is included in B2B contracts. We review every active permit, flag expirations, identify employees whose status doesn't match contracts, and prepare you for ZUS / PIP / urząd cudzoziemców inspections. Catches problems before regulators do.
What integrations does the dashboard support?
SSO via Google Workspace and Microsoft 365 (out of the box). REST API for custom integrations with your HRIS (BambooHR, Workday, SAP). Bulk CSV upload for legacy data. Slack/Teams notifications for status changes.
Can we use you for one-off VIP hires too?
Yes — alongside the contract you can flag specific cases as "Critical" — they get processed in 14 business days at 2× rate. Useful for senior hires you can't afford to lose. C-level / VP relocations frequently use this.
What happens to data if we end the contract?
All employee data is exported in JSON/CSV format and delivered to you. We retain only legally-mandated copies (anonymized) for 5 years per Polish law. GDPR-compliant deletion within 30 days of contract termination. You own your data.
Do you cover other EU countries beyond Poland?
We're Poland-focused for end-to-end legal work. For relocations from Poland to other EU states (Germany, Netherlands), we partner with vetted local firms and coordinate the handoff so your employee's permit transitions smoothly.
