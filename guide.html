The basics

What is a Karta Pobytu?

A Karta Pobytu (TRC — Temporary Residence Card) is the plastic card that proves you may legally live in Poland. It's tied to a reason: work, study, family or business. It lets you stay, work, travel in the Schengen zone and prove your status to any office, bank or employer.

📄 What it gives you

Legal residence for 1–3 years, the right to work, a PESEL, free Schengen travel, and a stable base for a future permanent permit (PRC) and citizenship.

⚠️ Why timing matters

If your current stay expires before you submit correctly, you risk a refusal, a re-entry ban, or losing your job. The process is procedural — small mistakes cost months.
