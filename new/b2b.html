Questions

Questions from HR and lawyers

What people usually ask before signing the contract.

What's the minimum volume for business terms?+
From 5 applications a year — that's the threshold for the base contract with a −10% discount. Most HR teams that regularly hire foreigners reach it within their first quarter. No "minimum fee" traps: you only pay for what we actually file.
How does the contract work?+
One annual contract that covers all your hires. The rate per case depends on your planned volume. Either party can terminate with 30 days' notice. The NDA covers all employee data. The price is locked for the year — if urząd fees change, we absorb the difference.
What if we fall short of the planned volume?+
No penalties. At year end the tier is recalculated based on actuals: if you planned 50 hires but did 25, you pay the tier "20+" rate (−25%) for those 25 cases, and next year we renegotiate based on the real numbers. We never invoice for cases that don't exist.
Do you run audits before government inspections?+
Yes — an annual compliance audit is included in the contract. We check every active permit, flag deadlines, find employees whose status doesn't match their umowa o pracę, and prepare you for inspections by ZUS, PIP and the foreigners' affairs office. We find problems before the regulator does.
How does the client portal work?+
For every employee — case status, a document checklist and the next step. We take new-hire data as a spreadsheet or a WhatsApp list — we don't dictate formats. Your manager reports every status change right away, and we prepare a summary report for leadership on request.
What about one-off VIP hires?+
Yes: alongside the contract, individual cases can be flagged as "critical" — we prepare and file their documents within 14 business days at double the rate; the urząd issues its decision on its own timeline. A lifesaver for key specialists you can't afford to lose.
What happens to the data if we terminate the contract?+
We export all employee data to JSON/CSV and hand it over to you — the data is yours. Under GDPR we delete everything within 30 days of termination. We keep anonymized copies for 5 years — Polish law requires it.
